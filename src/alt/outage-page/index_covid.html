2020 Teach Children to Save Day Moves Online!

Although the CEEE and the Delaware Financial Education Alliance in conjunction with the Delaware Bankers Association are unable to provide in-person classroom Teach Children to Save Day (TCTSD) presentations this year, we have created the next-best alternative: a digital offering that teachers can share with students and their families. Volunteers are encouraged to also share these resources with their own children, as well.

Enjoy a magical video lesson with The Great Investo as he reads the new book "The Great Investo and the Astounding, Abounding, Compounding Machine."

TCTSD is part of an annual, national financial literacy effort that works to encourage young students to develop the habits that will help ensure their future economic independence. In Delaware, the program typically invites bank volunteers to visit 3rd and 4th grade classrooms throughout the state to deliver a fun, engaging and valuable lesson on saving.  

We thank you all for the work that you're doing to create a positive remote learning environment for young learners, and for the important learning that will ensue. And we look forward to bringing this valuable program back to Delaware classrooms again next year.
